# VitalDiagnosis

The official repository of VitalDiagnosis: AI-Driven Ecosystem for 24/7 Vital Monitoring and Chronic Disease Management. We provide supplementary materials here.

## Overview

VitalDiagnosis is an advanced AI-powered healthcare platform designed to revolutionize chronic disease management through continuous vital sign monitoring and intelligent analysis. The system provides real-time health insights, enabling proactive intervention and personalized care management for patients with chronic conditions.

### Key Features

- **24/7 Vital Monitoring**: Continuous tracking of essential health metrics including heart rate, blood pressure, oxygen saturation, and more
- **AI-Driven Analysis**: Machine learning algorithms that detect patterns, predict health events, and provide actionable insights
- **Chronic Disease Management**: Specialized modules for managing conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and respiratory disorders
- **Real-Time Alerts**: Instant notifications for healthcare providers and patients when vital signs fall outside normal ranges
- **Personalized Care Plans**: Adaptive treatment recommendations based on individual patient data and medical history
- **Data Integration**: Seamless integration with various wearable devices and medical equipment
- **Secure & Compliant**: HIPAA-compliant data handling and storage to ensure patient privacy and security
